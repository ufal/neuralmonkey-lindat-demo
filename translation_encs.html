English-to-Czech Machine Translation

The machine translation model used in this demo is called the Transformer, originally developed by Google. This model was trained using the CzEng corpus, a parallel corpus consisting of 57 millions of English-Czech sentence pairs which makes it one of the biggest publicly available sources of machine translation data in the world. The training scheme for English-to-Czech translation from our institute used for this model was the winning submission to the WMT18 competition.
